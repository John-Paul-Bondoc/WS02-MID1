ABOUT DEKU

Izuku Midoriya, nicknamed Deku, is the main protagonist of the My Hero Academia franchise.

DEKU'S CHARACTERISTICS

  • He is dedicated to doing what he thinks is right and he strives to make the world a better place.
  • He is creative, imaginative, and empathetic.
  • He is a great listener. He prefers one-on-one situations rather than group activities.

DEKU'S QUIRK

He was born not possessing a quirk, but that doesn't stopped him from doing what he wants.. Being a hero. Number 1 Hero, 'All Might' would later give his quirk to Deku. The "One for All" quirk.
